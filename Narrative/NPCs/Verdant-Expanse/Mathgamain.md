# Mathgamain

#canon

**Location:** [[Verdant Expanse]] — village
**Faction:** [[Nemethari Culture|Nemethari]] (village elf)
**Role:** Merchant (smith), hidden lore
**Name meaning:** "Bear" in Old Irish — compound of *math* (bear) + *gamuin* (calf)

---

## Overview

A retired warrior who now works as the village smith. He hides his combat history from everyone, including the player — at least initially. He is good at his current trade, but his hands remember a different kind of work.

## Personality

Quiet, deliberate, uncomfortable with attention. He chose peace and does not want to be reminded of what came before. He deflects questions about his past with gruff practicality. He is not unfriendly — he is guarded.

## Function

- Village smith — provides equipment, repairs, crafting services
- Hidden depth: if the player earns his trust (through quests or repeated interaction), he reveals his past as a warrior and can provide combat-related knowledge or a unique item
- His dynamic with [[Cadfan]] provides ambient storytelling — the boy bullies him for being "weak," not knowing he is the most dangerous person in the village

## Key Dialogue Beats

Early interactions are transactional — he sells, he repairs, he says little. Over time, if the player returns and treats him with respect, he opens up. The reveal of his past should feel earned, not handed out.

## Relationships

- Bullied by [[Cadfan]], who calls him a weakling. The irony is visible to the player before it is confirmed.
- Respected by [[Tuathal]] as a peer.
- Keeps distance from the other villagers socially, though he cares about their safety.
