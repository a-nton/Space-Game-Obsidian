# Áine

#canon

**Location:** [[Verdant Expanse]] — village
**Faction:** [[Nemethari Culture|Nemethari]] (village elf)
**Role:** Lore source, ambient character
**Name meaning:** "Radiance, brilliance" (Old Irish)

---

## Overview

A young singer and flutist in the Verdant Expanse village. She knows the local tales and legends better than anyone her age — the kind of person who collects stories the way others collect tools.

## Appearance

Musical instruments close at hand. Expressive, animated when speaking about legends. Should read as someone who listens more than she talks, except when it comes to stories.

## Personality

Warm, curious, easily excited by new information. She treats stories as living things — not entertainment but heritage. She does not distinguish between "true" and "mythical" the way a scholar would. To her, the legends are true because they mean something, regardless of whether they happened.

## Function

- Hints at the existence of the Leviathan in the [[Faeblight Marsh]] through local legend
- Provides insight into elven cultures and beliefs — the spiritual layer that [[Tuathal]] dismisses
- Musical performances serve as ambient atmosphere in the village
- Potential early-game hook for the player to investigate the Marsh

## Key Dialogue Beats

She tells the player a story about a great beast that lives in the waters beneath the canopy. She doesn't know what it is — she calls it by a local name that predates the current generation's memory. If the player later encounters the Leviathan and returns, she is fascinated rather than frightened.

## Relationships

Contrast with [[Tuathal]], who dismisses the Leviathan as nonsense. Between the two of them, the player gets both the mythological and the sceptical perspective on the same creature.
