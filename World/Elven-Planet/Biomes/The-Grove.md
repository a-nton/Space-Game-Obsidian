# The Grove

#canon

**Planet:** Elven Planet (Nemethari Homeworld)
**Connects to:** [[Caer Bile]], [[Heartbloom Abattoir]]
**Hidden access to:** [[Verdant Expanse]] (secret section, descent only)
**Progression type:** Horizontal with elevation changes, slight downward slope

---

## Overview

A massive branch extending from the world tree, overgrown with moss and exposed to the constant rain on the planet. The Grove blocks that rain from falling on the [[Verdant Expanse]] below. This is wildling elf territory — a separate branch of Nemethari society even more intertwined with nature than the main population. They hivemind with the planet's flora and are symbiotically overgrown.

## Visual Identity

Wet, mossy, rain-soaked. Green and grey tones. Organic structures half-consumed by plant growth. The wildling settlements look like they were grown rather than built. Everything drips. The atmosphere should feel primal and unwelcoming — this is not the civilised elf world.

## Layout & Progression

Horizontal with a certain degree of elevation changes and a slight downward slope. Contains the wildling elf chief's ziggurat. The area connects to Caer Bile and to the Heartbloom Abattoir (accessible through catacombs below the ziggurat). Descending from certain points gives one-way access to the hidden section of the Verdant Expanse.

## Ecology

All enemies are non-hostile toward each other — they live in harmony with the wild elves and are autotrophic (drawing energy from the sun).

[[Clann Fiodhreime (Wild Elves)]] are hostile humanoid warriors fused with plant growth. They operate in coordinated groups, flanking and pressuring rather than rushing. If the player reaches and speaks to their elder, all wild elves become permanently non-hostile.

[[Daman Dealg (Bulb Deer)]] — thorn-covered, fat deer-like creature. Neutral unless attacked, then aggressive and durable. Can root itself to regenerate.

[[Gairfhiodhreime (Plant Fox)]] — coordinated pack predator. Calls allies with a howl. Hostility reduces but doesn't fully disappear after the area is pacified.

[[Blath Baisti (Bulb Mortar)]] — stationary plant turret. Small detection range, but once triggered, all nearby mortars begin firing. Can be snuck past or deaggro'd with stealth and patience.

[[Moss Sprites]] — passive floating creatures. Heal the player if they remain still nearby. Can be captured for crafting.

## NPCs

- Wildling Chieftain — the elder who can pacify the area. Speaking to them opens communication with the planet's flora itself. #unresolved — Can the chieftain save the planet?
- Flora spirit — accessible through the elder

## Lore Significance

The wildling elves are critical to the planet's salvation arc. When the civilisation enters its industrial phase and begins exploiting nature, the wildlings retreat into the Heartbloom Abattoir and go into hiding. They are living proof that the Nemethari can exist in true symbiosis with their world. Without them, the knowledge of that symbiosis is lost.
