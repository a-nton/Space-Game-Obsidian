# Verdant Expanse

#canon

**Planet:** Elven Planet (Nemethari Homeworld)
**Connects to:** [[Caer Bile]]
**One-way access from:** [[The Grove]] (to hidden section)
**Progression type:** Mostly linear, intro area

---

## Overview

Open plains full of stone circles and druidic markers — remnants of the original Nemethari civilisation. The area extends outward from [[Caer Bile]] with outposts and shacks of elven villagers branching out along a mostly linear path. Tall climbable flower-trees rise above dense grass. The ecosystem is active, populated by both flora and fauna.

## Visual Identity

Lush greens, warm natural light. Tall grass dominates the ground layer. Stone circles and carved druidic signs punctuate the landscape. Village outposts are modest, carved from living materials. The area should feel welcoming and pastoral, with underlying ecological tension visible to an attentive player.

## Layout & Progression

Mostly linear with branching exploration paths. Follows a natural route toward Caer Bile. A hidden section is accessible only by descending back into the Verdant Expanse from [[The Grove]]. This hidden area contains an abandoned Accord-era laboratory and a miniboss.

The hidden laboratory is one of the earliest clues that the planet has a history predating the Nemethari civilisation's memory. The architecture there should not match any known Nemethari craft.

## Ecology

A full predator-prey ecosystem. [[Fiafhreine (Moss Deer)]] graze in herds. [[Dealgshiul (Thorn Ambusher)]] hunt them from tall grass. [[Cu Dorchath (Rune Tiger)]] is a nocturnal apex stalker. [[Duibhear (Stone Ram)]] controls territory with lethal charges. [[Freamhghreim (Snare Trap)]] catches passing [[Leimfhear (Glowbug)|Leimfhear]] and unwary players. [[Luch Blatha (Petal Mice)]] serve as prey for everything and occasionally die visibly, telegraphing nearby predators.

Above it all patrols the [[Ard Fia na Fearach (Tree Giant)]] — the apex creature. Every other creature defers to it. Killing it makes the entire ecosystem permanently hostile.

## NPCs

- [[Aine]] — Singer, flutist. Knows local legends. Can hint at the Leviathan. Source of cultural and spiritual insight.
- [[Tuathal]] — Village elder. Knowledgeable about the forest and the hidden area. Dismisses the Leviathan as myth. Source of information on elven connection with nature and their lifespan.
- [[Mathgamain]] — Retired warrior, now a smithy. Hides his combat past. Bullied by Cadfan.
- [[Bebinn]] — Village girl. Loves flowers and dance. Friendly to kind visitors.
- [[Cadfan]] — Village boy. Aspires to be a warrior. Antagonises Mathgamain.
- [[Sithmaith]] — Village girl. Deeply bonded with animals. Accompanied by a chick (Ris) and a cat (Iudgual). Embodies the elven-fauna connection.

The hidden Accord laboratory (accessed via The Grove) contains the miniboss and early Alliance clues. Flag this prominently in environmental storytelling — it is the first breadcrumb suggesting the Accord existed.
