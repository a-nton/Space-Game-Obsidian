# Oillipheist Faebhrigh (Great Serpent)

#canon

**Biome:** [[Faeblight Marsh]]
**Tier:** Environmental hazard (unkillable)
**Behaviour type:** Reactive

---

## Overview

The apex creature of the Faeblight Marsh. A massive serpent-like leviathan that resides beneath the glowing river. Cannot be killed. Its presence defines the entire biome's movement rules: if you are in the water, you are dead.

## Ecology

Constantly moves below the surface, occasionally visible as a large shadow passing underneath. Hunts [[Each Uisce (Sea Horse)]] and anything else that enters the water. A very similar creature to the most common pets on the fish planet — strongly hinting that this monster was a scientist's pet, left behind when the [[The Accord|Accord]] fell.

## Behaviour

Detects disturbances in the water. If the player falls in, it immediately rushes toward them and consumes them. It does not patrol aggressively — it waits. The threat is the water itself.

## Cross-Planet Gate

If the player acquires the special traversal item from another planet (silent movement in water), the Great Serpent no longer reacts, allowing safe passage to deeper areas. At the lake bottom: submerged structures clearly not built by the [[Nemethari Culture|Nemethari]]. Their architectural style matches a different Accord species.

This is the first cross-planet Metroidvania gate in the game.

## Audio

Distant rumbling beneath the water surface. The shadow passing underneath should be accompanied by a low-frequency tremor. Silence when the water is still — making the player hyperaware of their own movement sounds.
