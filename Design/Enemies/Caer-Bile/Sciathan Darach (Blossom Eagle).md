# Sciathan Darach (Blossom Eagle)

#canon

**Biome:** [[Caer Bile]]
**Tier:** Elite (environmental threat)
**Behaviour type:** Hostile (audio-cue stealth mechanic)

---

## Overview

Gliding aerial predator. Resides in mid-to-upper sections of Caer Bile. Perches above platforms and drops onto prey. The defining stealth mechanic of the hub area.

## Ecology

Hunts [[Braonan (Parasite Slug)]] primarily. Will also attack other small critters if no other target is present. Has a nest as a side area near the top of Caer Bile.

## Behaviour — Audio Cue Mechanic

The eagle sounds an audio cue every 20–30 seconds. If the player remains in the open for more than 3 seconds after the cue is heard, the eagle swoops in and kills instantly.

The mechanic is introduced to the player by watching the eagle swoop in and kill a parasite slug first. When the audio cue plays, all small creatures run toward nearby tall grass or cover — teaching the player the correct response through observation.

## Attacks & Abilities

One-hit kill swoop. No melee phase. It either kills or it doesn't engage.

## Weaknesses

Predictable timing once the player learns the audio cue interval. Cover and tall grass provide complete safety.

## Special Interaction

If the player has a [[Braonan (Parasite Slug)]] attached, the eagle will target the slug on the player's body. In this case, its attack removes the slug without damaging the player. This can be used in puzzles — deliberately carrying a parasite slug into the eagle's territory to have it removed.

## Audio

The audio cue is the most important sound design element in Caer Bile. It must be distinct, audible over ambient sound, and produce an immediate urgency response. The swoop itself should be near-silent — a rush of wind, not a screech.
