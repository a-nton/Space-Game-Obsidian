# Fiafhreine (Moss Deer)

#canon

**Biome:** [[Verdant Expanse]]
**Tier:** Standard
**Behaviour type:** Neutral (hostile when cornered)

---

## Overview

Deer-like creature with moss fur and twig antlers. Glowing rock hooves. The most abundant creature in the Verdant Expanse.

## Ecology

Herbivore. Moves in herds. Primary prey for [[Dealgshiul (Thorn Ambusher)]] and [[Cu Dorchath (Rune Tiger)]]. Avoids tall grass (because of the Dealgshiul), which provides the player a safe zone if hiding from the herd. Default behaviour is idling and grazing.

## Behaviour

Non-hostile unless attacked. If one is attacked, the whole herd attempts to flee. If cornered, the herd charges the player. Very tanky — difficult to gun down a full group. If the player hides in tall grass, the entire group runs away.

## Attacks & Abilities

Charge attack when cornered. Dangerous in numbers due to combined health pool.

## Weaknesses

Avoids tall grass. Can be scattered by retreating into it. Individually not threatening — the danger is engaging the herd without an escape route.

## Visuals

Tree deer silhouette. Moss fur, twig antlers, glowing rock hooves. Reads as gentle and pastoral until provoked.

## Audio

Soft ambient grazing sounds. Alarm call when one is attacked. Thundering hooves during charge.

## Design Notes

The Fiafhreine's relationship with the Dealgshiul creates a visible predator-prey dynamic the player can observe. Their avoidance of tall grass telegraphs the ambusher's presence.

When the [[Ard Fia na Fearach (Tree Giant)]] is on screen, the Fiafhreine perform a bowing or calming animation — acknowledging the apex creature.
