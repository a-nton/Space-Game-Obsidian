# The Athnemeton

#canon

**Pronunciation:** ath-NEM-eh-ton — from *ath-* (again, re-) + *nemeton* (sacred grove). "The grove remade."

---

The Athnemeton are the [[Nemethari Elder|Elder's]] attempt to recreate his people. They are what he remembered, and memory is not faithful.

## Origins

The planet they inhabit was constructed by the Elder as camouflage against the [[The Beholder|Beholder]]. Everything on its surface is derived from the Elder's fading memories of the [[Nemethari Culture|original Nemethari homeworld]], filtered through millennia of isolation, grief, and diminishing cognition. The result is a world that resembles the original the way a half-remembered dream resembles the day that produced it. The shapes are right. The colours are too vivid. The logic connecting one thing to another has come apart.

## Appearance

The Elder's memories were strongest for one individual: his sibling, an elder named **Athgeni** (ath-GEN-ee, meaning "reborn"). Her specific appearance — her jewellery, the way she carried herself — became the template for the entire species. Every Athnemeton carries echoes of her face. Their adornments are fused into their bodies rather than worn, as though someone tried to recreate Athgeni from her outline without knowing which part was skin and which part was gold. The silhouettes are smeared. Up close, the distinction between organism and ornament has dissolved entirely.

## Architecture

Buildings are shaped like trees, burrows, and forest structures, but they bear no functional relationship to the actual terrain. A dwelling shaped like a hollow oak might stand on open grassland with no forest in sight. The forms are remembered in isolation from the landscapes that once gave them purpose.

## Rituals

The Athnemeton perform ceremonies that mirror the original Nemethari seasonal rites (Samhain, Imbolc, Beltane, Lughnasadh) but no one can explain what they are for. They are performed out of biological compulsion — the way a person hums a melody they cannot place. The gestures survive. The meaning is gone.

## Colour

The same palette as the original Nemethari world, but pushed toward saturation and high contrast. The way you recall a place you loved: the greens are greener, the light is warmer, the proportions are grander than they actually were. The Elder's world is his homeworld as elegy.

## Factions

The Athnemeton are divided into two groups:

- **[[Cethrai]]** (KETH-rye) — those connected to the energy cycle. Crystals intact. Recycled every 100 years.
- **[[Dechrai]]** (DEKH-rye) — those severed from the cycle. Must sustain themselves biologically. Feared and hunted.
