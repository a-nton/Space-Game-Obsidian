# Energy Cycle and Soul-Gems

#canon

---

Every living creature on the [[Nemethari Elder|Elder's]] planet is connected to his energy cycle through a green crystalline formation lodged in their body. For the humanoid [[Athnemeton]], this crystal sits in the chest. It functions as a conduit: the creature draws sustenance from the planet's energy field through the crystal, and at the end of its hundred-year span the crystal releases the accumulated energy back into the field. The creature dissolves. A new one forms. The cycle repeats without interruption.

## Purpose

The hundred-year cap exists because the Elder designed the cycle as a signal-scattering mechanism. Each individual creature acts as a node in a distributed network that masks the Elder's cognitive signature from the [[The Beholder|Beholder]]. If any single node accumulated too much energy for too long, it would begin to emit a detectable concentration spike. The recycling prevents this.

The original [[Nemethari Culture|Nemethari]] lifespan was far longer — around 10,000 years. The Elder's imposed 100-year cap is a massive compression.

#unresolved — Do the Athnemeton know their natural lifespan would be longer? Or is 100 years simply "how long things live" to them? This affects the emotional register of the [[Dechrai]]'s existence. If the Athnemeton have no concept of a longer life, the Dechrai are living proof that the world is lying to them.

## Samsara Parallel

In practice, this is samsara as engineering. The recycling of souls through the energy cycle, the capping of individual lifespans to prevent accumulation of too much energy or knowledge, the compulsory nature of the process. In Hindu and Buddhist cosmology, the cycle of birth and death is sustained by a cosmic mechanism (karma, maya, ignorance) that prevents beings from accumulating enough awareness to escape. The Elder built the same mechanism deliberately. His motivation was survival rather than malice, but the result is identical. Beings are born, live, dissolve, and reform without continuity.

## The Dechrai Exception

Those whose crystals have been lost, broken, or deliberately removed are severed from the cycle. They must eat, drink, and breathe. They are not recycled. They can live for thousands of years. See [[Dechrai]].

## Parasitic Corruption

When the [[Sreng|parasites]] reach the planet, they infiltrate the energy cycle. The [[Cethrai]]'s crystals, normally green, begin tinting toward blue-purple — the parasites' chromatic signature. Behaviour shifts follow: distance, erratic movement, fragmented speech, prolonged silence.

The Dechrai, disconnected from the cycle, are immune. The quality that makes them outcasts is what protects them.

This irony should be a pivotal narrative beat. The people the Cethrai have called parasites for generations are the only ones unaffected by the actual parasites.
