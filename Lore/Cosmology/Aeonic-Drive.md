# Aeonic Drive

#canon

---

The Aeonic Drive was the [[The Accord|Accord's]] attempt to create a self-sustaining energy source powerful enough to eliminate scarcity across all six systems. Each of the six species contributed one Discipline — a component designed within their area of scientific expertise.

The Drive required a seventh element to unify the six Disciplines into a stable whole. That element was [[The Vessel]], originally engineered as a biomechanical housing shell capable of interfacing with every Discipline simultaneously.

The Drive's test detonation failed catastrophically, destroying one system's planet and sending a shockwave into the outer void — which drew the [[The Beholder|Beholder]].

## The Amplification Core

The Nemethari contribution. Designed to magnify any energy input by orders of magnitude. The [[Nemethari Elder]] later used it on himself, triggering an uncontrolled ascension that expanded his physical and cognitive capacities far beyond biological limits. The Core was never meant to interface with a living body.

After the Elder's final act of self-preservation, the Core shattered. The Vessel integrated the largest remaining shard, which retained the Core's essential property: it amplifies any energy it receives. Fed by even a trace of the planet's life-field, the shard produces enough power to sustain the Vessel indefinitely. This is why the player never needs to refuel.

## The Seventh Component

The Vessel itself is the seventh piece. It was built to interface with all six Disciplines simultaneously, and no other construct in the galaxy can do this. The "seventh piece" is not a separate object but the Vessel reconfigured into a bridge that harmonises the six Disciplines into a stable whole.

The player should not learn they are the seventh component until late in the game. The dramatic weight of this revelation depends on the player having spent most of the journey believing the seventh piece is something they still need to find.
