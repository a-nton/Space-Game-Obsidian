# The Accord

#canon

---

Six intelligent species evolved independently across a single galaxy. Over millennia they discovered one another, traded knowledge, and eventually formed a scientific coalition. Each species excelled in a different domain of research. Their combined efforts converged on a single ambition: creating a self-sustaining energy source powerful enough to make scarcity irrelevant across all six systems.

Each star system was tasked with developing expertise in a distinct scientific discipline and producing its corresponding component of the [[Aeonic Drive]]. Near completion, the Accord's calculations were found to be flawed and the core was unstable. The experiment detonated, leaving one system's planet a scorched ruin.

The explosion generated sufficient force to penetrate the planet's crust. Its shockwave proceeded outward beyond the system's boundary, past the neighbouring dead stars. After years of travelling through empty space and slowly decaying, it reached the outer void as little more than a residual tremor in the radiation background.

The void had been still for a very long time. Even that faint tremor meant something.

## The Six Disciplines

Each species contributed one component to the Drive. The Nemethari contribution was the Amplification Core, designed to magnify any energy input by orders of magnitude.

#unresolved — The other five species and their Disciplines have not yet been defined. Each planet the player visits corresponds to one species and one Discipline.

## Aftermath

The [[The Beholder|Beholder]] entered the galaxy's centre and observed. It determined that the six species were developed enough to be worth harvesting, and that the Drive project, though it had failed, represented a threat that could be reattempted. It chose to drain the Accord's intellect and ensure the Drive could never be completed.

The Beholder selected one representative from each species and enslaved them as [[The Overseers]]. It also salvaged the Accord's surviving technology, repurposing assistant and combat machines from all six species into a robotic extraction force.

## Design Notes

The name "Accord" is what the [[The Vessel|Vessel's]] translation matrix defaults to. Each species called the coalition something different in their own language.
